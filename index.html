My Top 3 Animated Movies Of the 21st Century

  • Zootopia

    Synopsis: A rabbit named Judy moves to the big city to become a police officer. She wants to prove that she is brave even though she is small. Judy teams up with a fox named Nick to solve a mystery. They find out that some animals are going missing for a secret reason. In the end, they save the city and become a team.

  • Coraline

    Synopsis: A young girl named Coraline moves into an old house and feels bored. She finds a secret door that leads to a different, magical version of her life. At first, everything seems perfect, but the "Other Mother" wants to keep Coraline forever. Coraline must be brave to save her real parents and escape the trap. She learns that home is where she truly belongs.

  • How to Train Your Dragon

    Synopsis: Hiccup is a young Viking who does not fit in because he is not a strong fighter. Instead of killing a dragon, he makes friends with a dangerous Night Fury named Toothless. Hiccup learns that dragons are misunderstood creatures and trains Toothless. He shows his village that Vikings and dragons can live together happily. Finally, Hiccup becomes a hero and brings peace to his island.
